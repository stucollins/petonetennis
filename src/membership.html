Senior Membership
If you are interested in becoming a senior member than come along to one of our senior club days on Saturday or Sunday from 1pm. You can meet the members and try out the club.

Annual membership is one of the cheapest in the Wellington and Hutt Valley area. For a small amount extra you can also get a key to the club house and courts so you can get access to the courts when ever you feel like a game of tennis.
Junior Membership and Coaching
The club also has a large junior membership with coaching on Saturday mornings, junior club night on Wed nights and junior interclub practice on Tuesday nights.
Please contact us if you would like further information
